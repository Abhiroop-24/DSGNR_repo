Ready to join the gallery?

DSGNR is reserved for verified members of the art and design club at PES University EC Campus. Reach out to the club leads to request your credentials.

  • • Capture project journeys from sketches to final renders.
  • • Collaborate on design challenges and themed events.
  • • Showcase studio stories, murals, and community initiatives.

How to access

  1. Fill out the access request with your institute email.
  2. Club admins verify membership and set up your profile.
  3. Log in to upload artwork and explore the DSGNR feed.
